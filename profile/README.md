## 🚀 Summary
Sierra Chart for Windows is a desktop platform built for traders who put charting and order flow first. It handles large volumes of historical and real-time data with very little lag, so charts stay quick even when you have many symbols and studies running. Alongside candlestick and tick charts, it offers footprint (numbers bars) charts for close order flow reading, a large indicator library, and support for custom studies. It is lightweight, reliable through long sessions, and deeply configurable for any active trading routine.

## ✨ Feature Set
1. **Footprint order flow charts** — See traded volume at every price level to follow how orders fill inside each bar. 📊
2. **High-performance rendering** — Keep charts fast and responsive even with large datasets and many open windows. ⚡
3. **Extensive indicators** — Apply a wide range of Sierra Chart indicators and technical studies out of the box. 🧮
4. **Custom study support** — Build and add your own studies to extend the platform to your exact needs. 🛠️
5. **Configurable trading tools** — Enter and manage orders from the chart with layouts and controls you can tailor. 🎛️
